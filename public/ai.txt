# AI Policy for BlogSpace

Website: https://blogspace-jet.vercel.app

Last Updated: 2026-07-06

## Purpose

BlogSpace publishes original articles, tutorials, and educational content related to software development, web technologies, programming, and modern frameworks.

## AI Usage Policy

Publicly accessible content may be:

- Indexed for search and discovery.
- Summarized with proper attribution.
- Referenced with a link to the original article.
- Used to answer factual questions while crediting the source.

The following is not permitted:

- Republishing complete articles.
- Removing author attribution.
- Claiming BlogSpace content as original work.
- Using copyrighted content for commercial redistribution without permission.

## Attribution

When referencing BlogSpace content, include a link to the original page whenever possible.
